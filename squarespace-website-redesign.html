FAQ

Questions buyers ask before they book.

How much does a Squarespace redesign cost?

Squarespace redesigns start at $2,250 for a focused homepage refresh, $3,750 for a full site rebuild, and $5,000+ when the project needs deeper strategy, content, integrations, or location pages.

Can you preserve my existing Squarespace URLs?

Yes. We map the current URL structure, keep what should stay, redirect what changes, and protect the pages that already have visibility.

Is a custom site better than Squarespace?

For serious service businesses and professional firms, usually yes. A custom hand-coded site gives more control over performance, schema, layout, tracking, and conversion flow.

Can I still request edits after launch?

Yes. You can use a site care plan for updates, or own the code and use another developer if you prefer. No lock-in.

How long does the rebuild take?

Most Squarespace replacements have a first draft in 72 hours and launch in 7 to 10 days once content and final approvals are ready.
