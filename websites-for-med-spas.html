FAQ

Common questions from med spa owners.

Answers to what we hear from injectors, owners, and practice managers when they reach out.

How much does a med spa website cost?

Med spa web design at TMN Creative starts at $2,250 for a Starter Refresh, $3,750 for a Full Site Rebuild (multi-page site with treatment menu, provider bios, before/after galleries, memberships, financing, online booking, and SEO), and $5,000+ for Custom Studio Builds (multi-location med spa groups, luxury aesthetic studios, integrated dermatology/plastic surgery practices). Flat fees, no retainers, no monthly hosting lock-in. Optional site care plans run $39 to $179 per month.

Do you integrate with Boulevard, Mindbody, Vagaro, and AestheticRecord?

Yes. We integrate the marketing site with Boulevard, Mindbody, Vagaro, AestheticRecord, Joya, Nextech, PatientNow, Symplast, Aesthetic Pro, and most modern med spa and aesthetic practice software. Booking widgets, membership signups, and lead capture all push directly into your scheduling system. We also wire in patient communication tools like Klara, Weave, and Solutionreach where applicable, so review pulls and recall messaging stay in sync with the same patient record.

How do you handle before/after galleries with patient consent?

Carefully. Before/after galleries are the highest-converting content on a med spa site, but they have to be handled with proper consent and compliance in mind. We build a structured B/A gallery system with consent metadata per image, treatment notes, units used (where applicable for Botox), provider attribution, and Schema.org ImageObject markup. We also build admin workflows so your team can pull or anonymize images quickly if a patient revokes consent — which is critical for protecting both the patient and the practice.

Are TMN Creative med spa websites HIPAA aware?

Yes. We design lead forms and patient intake to be HIPAA-aware: minimal PHI on the marketing site, BAA-eligible form processors (HIPAA-compliant providers like Jotform Healthcare, Cognito Forms HIPAA, Formidable HIPAA), encryption in transit and at rest, no PHI in URL parameters, no PHI in plain analytics, server-side event de-identification. Patient intake forms with sensitive info typically route to a HIPAA-compliant intake portal in your practice software (AestheticRecord, PatientNow, Boulevard, etc.), not the marketing site.

How do you display treatment menus and pricing?

We build a structured treatment menu system: each treatment (Botox, filler, microneedling, CoolSculpting, IV drips, semaglutide, etc.) gets a dedicated page with what it does, how long it takes, recovery, ideal candidates, and either pricing or a clear booking CTA. For practices that prefer to keep prices off the public site, we offer protected pricing pages that unlock after a consult booking. Schema.org Service markup helps treatment pages rank for high-intent searches like "Botox cost Fort Worth" or "Morpheus8 near me."

Can the site sell memberships and recurring packages?

Yes. We build dedicated landing pages for monthly aesthetic memberships (Alle, Aspire Galderma, Xperience+, or custom in-house programs), unlimited Hydrafacial memberships, monthly Botox plans, weight loss programs, and IV therapy memberships. Online enrollment with recurring billing via Stripe, Boulevard, or AestheticRecord native subscription tools turns the site into a real recurring revenue engine. Membership signups from the site are forecastable, predictable, and dramatically reduce no-show rates.

How long does it take to build a med spa website?

First draft of the homepage in 72 hours. Full med spa sites are typically live in 7 to 10 days. Custom Studio Builds for multi-location groups, luxury aesthetic studios, and integrated dermatology/plastic surgery practices take 2 to 3 weeks. We handle photo curation, treatment copy, provider bio writing, and SEO copy in that timeline so your team does not have to write everything themselves.

Can my med spa website be understood by ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews?

Yes. Every med spa site we build is optimized for AI search (AIO) alongside traditional SEO. Structured data (MedicalBusiness, BeautySalon, Service, FAQ schema), question-and-answer content blocks, properly cited service area information, and crawlable architecture mean ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews can better understand your business when prospective patients ask "who is the best med spa for Botox in [city]?" or "where can I get Morpheus8 near me?" — a growing part of how patients research aesthetic providers.
