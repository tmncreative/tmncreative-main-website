What's Included

Built into every healthcare site we ship.

Healthcare websites have to do more than look good. They have to load fast on a phone in a parking lot, route patients to the right next step, and respect HIPAA without making the site feel cold. Here is what we build into every healthcare site, by default, with no upcharges.

01

HIPAA-aware design

Marketing-site forms stay PHI-free. Patient intake routes to your HIPAA-covered EHR or scheduling system (Jane, SimplePractice, Healthie, Dentrix, Eaglesoft, AthenaHealth, NextGen, ChartLogic). No protected info gets logged in analytics or stored on the marketing site.

02

Mobile-first performance

Hand-coded HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No WordPress bloat, no page builders. Healthcare builds target strong Lighthouse and Core Web Vitals scores with fast mobile loads on 4G — which is how many patients find you.

03

Provider bios that build trust

Every provider gets a clean bio page with credentials, education, board certifications, training, and a real photograph. Schema markup tags each provider as a Physician entity so Google and AI search engines understand who works at your practice.

04

Treatment & service pages

Each major service gets its own page — not buried in a dropdown. We write copy that addresses the actual patient concern (pain, cost, recovery, what to expect) rather than clinical jargon. This is what gets indexed, understood, and converted.

05

Local SEO foundations

Schema markup for MedicalBusiness and LocalBusiness, NAP consistency across pages, embedded maps, location-specific landing pages for multi-office practices, optimized Google Business Profile guidance, and Apple Maps + Bing Places coverage.

06

AI-search structure

Every page we ship is structured for AI-assisted search tools. Clear answers, comprehensive FAQ schema, llms.txt support, and entity-rich copy. This helps patients and search systems understand the practice faster.

07

Online booking integration

We integrate with whatever scheduler you already pay for — Jane, SimplePractice, Calendly, Healthie, NexHealth, Mindbody, Vagaro, Booker, Acuity, or your EHR's native booking. Patients book in two taps.

08

Accessibility (WCAG 2.2 AA)

Healthcare practices have higher exposure to ADA web accessibility complaints. Every site is built against WCAG 2.2 AA accessibility practices: color contrast, keyboard navigation, screen reader semantics, focus states, and form labels. We also include an accessibility statement page.

09

You own everything

Code, copy, design files, hosting access, domain. Everything transfers to you on day one. No retainers, no hostage hosting, no proprietary CMS lock-in. If you ever leave, you take the site with you.

FAQ

Common questions from healthcare practices.

Answers to the questions we hear from medical, dental, med spa, and therapy practices when they first reach out.

How much does a healthcare website cost?

Healthcare web design at TMN Creative starts at $2,250 for a Starter Refresh (homepage redesign), $3,750 for a Full Site Rebuild (full multi-page practice site), and $5,000+ for a Custom Studio Build (multi-location practices and surgical groups). All prices are flat fees with no retainers. Optional site care runs $39 to $179 per month for hosting, monitoring, and updates.

How do TMN Creative healthcare websites handle HIPAA-sensitive flows?

Yes — we build HIPAA-aware sites. Marketing-site forms stay free of protected health information (PHI). Patient intake routes to your existing HIPAA-covered EHR or scheduling system (Jane, SimplePractice, Healthie, Dentrix, AthenaHealth, NextGen, etc.). No PHI is logged in analytics. Site-wide TLS encryption is enabled by default. We work alongside your privacy officer or compliance counsel to ensure intake flows respect your protected-health-information policies.

How long does it take to build a healthcare website?

First paid draft in 72 hours. Full site live in 7 to 10 days for most healthcare practices. Custom Studio Builds for multi-location practices, surgical groups, or compliance-heavy operators take 2 to 3 weeks.

What healthcare specialties does TMN Creative work with?

Medical practices, dental offices, med spas and aesthetic clinics, therapy and counseling groups, chiropractic, optometry, physical therapy, integrative and functional medicine, concierge and direct primary care, dermatology, plastic surgery, women's health, IV therapy, weight loss clinics, fertility clinics, vein clinics, pain management, podiatry, and veterinary practices.

Do you do healthcare SEO and AI-search structure?

Yes. Every healthcare site we build includes traditional SEO foundations (MedicalBusiness and Physician schema, local SEO, optimized meta tags, fast Core Web Vitals, semantic HTML, internal linking) and AI-search structure (FAQ schema, llms.txt, entity-rich copy, structured answers). Patients increasingly compare providers through search and AI tools; we make the page easy to read without guessing.

Can you build a website for a multi-location practice?

Yes. Multi-location practices are a common Custom Studio Build. We build location-specific landing pages with local schema, distinct provider bios per office, location-specific reviews, mapping integrations, and a consistent visual system across the network. Pricing starts at $5,000.

What features should a modern healthcare website have?

A modern healthcare website should include: clear treatment and service pages (one per major service), transparent pricing where regulations allow, provider bios with credentials and headshots, online booking or HIPAA-aware intake routing, mobile-first responsive design, Fast mobile performance, schema markup for MedicalBusiness and Physician entities, WCAG 2.2 AA accessibility, secure form handling, and copy that addresses real patient concerns rather than clinical jargon.

Where is TMN Creative located, and do you work with practices outside Texas?

TMN Creative is based in Fort Worth, Texas. We work remotely with healthcare practices across the United States — Texas, Arizona, California, New York, and beyond. Most client communication happens by Zoom, email, and shared design files. Onsite visits are available for Custom Studio Build engagements.
