Mobile-First Design

Most websites are designed backwards. Desktop first, mobile as an afterthought.

Your customers are searching on their phones. They are sitting in their car, standing in their kitchen with a leaking pipe, or scrolling through Instagram when your ad catches their eye. The first thing they see is your mobile site. If it is slow, cramped, or hard to navigate, they bounce.

Mobile-first means we design the phone experience before anything else. Thumb-friendly tap targets. Click-to-call buttons that are impossible to miss. Forms that work with one hand. Images optimized to load fast on cellular connections. Text sized so nobody has to pinch and zoom.

Then we scale up to tablet and desktop, adding more visual space where it is available. The result is a site that works perfectly on every device, with the mobile experience driving the design decisions instead of being an afterthought.

Every site we build at TMN Creative is mobile-first. It is not an add-on or an upgrade. It is how we work.
