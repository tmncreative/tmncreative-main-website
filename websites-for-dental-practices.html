FAQ

Common questions from dental practices.

Answers to what we hear from dentists, practice managers, and DSO operators when they reach out.

How much does a dental practice website cost?

Dental practice web design at TMN Creative starts at $2,250 for a Starter Refresh, $3,750 for a Full Site Rebuild (multi-page site with provider bios, procedure pages, online booking, before/after galleries, insurance, financing, ADA compliance), and $5,000+ for Custom Studio Builds (DSOs and multi-location groups). Flat fees, no retainers, no monthly hosting lock-in. Optional site care plans run $39 to $179 per month if you want us handling updates and tweaks ongoing.

Do you integrate with Dentrix, Eaglesoft, Open Dental, and NexHealth?

Yes. We integrate with Dentrix, Eaglesoft, Open Dental, NexHealth, LocalMed, Yapi, Modento, Curve Dental, Practice-Web, and most modern dental practice management systems for online booking. We do not store PHI on the marketing site — patients book through your existing system, which keeps the marketing site outside HIPAA scope while still providing one-click booking. We can also wire in patient communication tools like Weave, Dental Intel, RevenueWell, and Solutionreach for review pulls and recall messaging.

Are TMN Creative dental websites built with accessibility best practices?

Yes. Every dental practice site we build meets WCAG 2.2 AA standards: color contrast, keyboard navigation, screen reader semantics, focus states, and form labels. Each site includes an accessibility statement page. Dental practices have higher exposure to ADA web accessibility complaints than most local businesses (serial filers actively target them), so we treat accessibility as table stakes — not a paid upgrade. We also test with axe DevTools and manual keyboard navigation before every site goes live.

Do you replace DentalQore, ProSites, PBHS, or Officite sites?

Yes — frequently. Most dental sites we replace are DentalQore, ProSites, PBHS, Officite, Smile Marketing, Pro Impressions, Roadside Dental Marketing, or My Social Practice templates. Those platforms ship cookie-cutter sites with monthly hosting lock-in (often $300-$700/month) and limited code access. Our hand-coded sites load much faster, look distinct (not templated), are built for stronger Core Web Vitals, and are fully owned by you. We handle the migration, redirects, and SEO continuity to protect as much existing visibility as possible during the move.

Can you build a website for a multi-location dental practice or DSO?

Yes. Multi-location dental practices and DSOs are a common Custom Studio Build. We build per-location landing pages with local schema, distinct provider bios per office, location-specific Google reviews, mapping integrations, location-aware online booking that routes to the right calendar, and a consistent visual system across the network. Pricing starts at $5,000 and scales with location count. We have shipped builds for groups ranging from 3 locations to 30+, including DSOs with regional brand systems.

How long does it take to build a dental practice website?

First draft of the homepage in 72 hours. Full dental practice sites are typically live in 7 to 10 days. Multi-location DSO builds take 2 to 3 weeks. We also handle photo selection, provider bio copywriting, procedure page content, and SEO copy in that timeline so your team does not have to write everything themselves. We have launched practices in as little as 5 days when timing matters (post-merger rebrands, lease-driven moves).

Can my dental practice website be understood by ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews?

Yes. Every dental practice site we build is optimized for AI search (AIO) alongside traditional SEO. Structured data (Dentist, MedicalBusiness, Person, FAQ schema), question-and-answer content, properly cited service area information, and crawlable architecture mean ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews can better understand your business when patients ask "who is the best dentist in [city]?" or "who does same-day crowns near me?" — increasingly the front door for new-patient searches before they ever Google.

What dental specialties does TMN Creative work with?

General dentistry, cosmetic dentistry, orthodontics (traditional and clear aligner), pediatric dentistry, oral surgery, periodontics, endodontics, prosthodontics, implant specialists, sleep dentistry, sedation dentistry, family dentistry, and dental service organizations (DSOs). Each specialty gets purpose-built procedure pages, referring-doctor workflows where applicable, and content tuned to how patients (and referring GPs) actually search.
