FAQ

Before you reach out.

Have another question? Write it in the form below.

01
How do you build sites this fast?

You work directly with the people building your site. No middlemen, no hand-offs between teams. Most clients see their first design in 72 hours and a fully launched site within seven days of kickoff.

02
What if my site just needs a refresh, not a full rebuild?

That is what the $2,250 Starter Refresh covers. Homepage redesign, copy cleanup, mobile optimization. Live within a week. If you want to expand later, everything carries forward into a Full Rebuild.

03
What does the Operations Platform actually do?

It replaces the bloated CRM your team half-uses. We build a custom pipeline, tasks, billing view, and reporting dashboard around your actual workflow. Gmail, Calendar, and Stripe sync live. Everything stays in one place, and you own it.

04
Do I need a new website to get the platform?

No. The platform runs independent of your website. That said, most clients find a sharp site paired with a real operations tool is where things compound. That is why the bundle exists.

05
What’s the monthly fee for?

Hosting, uptime monitoring, backups, ongoing optimization, and updates as your workflow changes. 90-day minimum, then month to month. No long-term contracts.

06
Will I be able to update the site myself?

Yes. Site Care plans run from $39/mo for hosting and security up to $179/mo for priority support and monthly updates, for operators who would rather not touch the site at all.

07
Starter Refresh or Full Rebuild?

Starter Refresh is a single homepage redesign for $2,250. Full Rebuild starts at $3,750 and includes deeper positioning, multi-page structure, and SEO. We scope everything on the strategy call. No pressure to upgrade.

08
Do you build websites for healthcare, RIAs, and law firms?

Yes. Regulated industries are our specialty. Healthcare practices get HIPAA-aware design with PHI-free marketing forms. RIAs and financial advisors get FINRA Rule 2210 and SEC Marketing Rule compliance-aware copy with proper ADV/Form CRS hosting. Law firms get ABA Model Rule 7-aware copy with required disclaimers. We work alongside your CCO, privacy officer, or compliance counsel to support your review process.

09
What technologies do you build with?

Hand-coded HTML, CSS, and JavaScript hosted on Netlify. No WordPress, no Wix, no Squarespace, no Webflow, no page builders. This makes our sites 3 to 5 times faster than template platforms, fully owned by you, and not vulnerable to plugin attacks. Custom operations platforms run on modern stacks (Next.js, Postgres, Stripe, Gmail/Google Calendar APIs) tailored to the use case.

10
Do you do SEO and AI search optimization?

Yes. Every site we ship includes traditional SEO foundations (semantic HTML, schema markup including Organization, LocalBusiness, Service, FAQPage, optimized meta tags, fast Core Web Vitals, internal linking, XML sitemap) and AI search optimization (FAQ schema, llms.txt, entity-rich copy, structured answers) for ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews. We have documented increasing lead volume from AI search engines — we build pages that are easier for those systems to understand and cite when selected.

11
Where is TMN Creative located?

TMN Creative is based in Fort Worth, Texas. We work remotely with clients across the United States — Texas, Arizona, California, New York, and beyond. Most communication happens by Zoom, email, and shared design files. Onsite visits are available for Custom Studio Build engagements.

12
Do I own the code, copy, and design after launch?

Yes. Code, copy, design files, domain, and hosting access all transfer to you on day one. No proprietary CMS, no hostage hosting, no retainer. If you ever leave us, you take the site with you. This is a deliberate counter to template platforms (DentalQore, ProSites, Justia, FindLaw, Scorpion) that lock clients in via monthly hosting.

13
How does TMN Creative compare to a typical web design agency?

A typical agency: 8 to 12 weeks, $15K to $50K, 4-person team between you and the work, WordPress with 30+ plugins, monthly retainer required, you don’t fully own the code. TMN Creative: 7 to 10 days, $2,250 to $5,000, founders build it directly, hand-coded HTML/CSS, no retainer, you own everything. We are smaller, faster, and meaningfully cheaper because we cut everything that isn’t building or shipping.
