FAQ

Common questions from home services operators.

Answers to what we hear from HVAC, plumbing, landscaping, and other home services owners when they reach out to TMN Creative.

How much does a home services website cost?

Home services web design at TMN Creative starts at $2,250 for a Starter Refresh, $3,750 for a Full Site Rebuild (multi-page site with service-area pages, scheduling integration, financing, reviews, local SEO), and $5,000+ for Custom Studio Builds (multi-location operators, multi-trade companies, franchises). Flat fees, no retainers. Optional site care plans run $39 to $179 per month for monitoring, updates, and on-call edits.

Do you integrate with ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro, and Jobber?

Yes. TMN Creative integrates with ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro, Jobber, Workiz, FieldEdge, Service Fusion, ServiceBox, and most modern home services field service management platforms. Customers can book service, request estimates, or fill out lead forms that drop straight into your dispatch board. We can also wire in Google Local Services Ads (LSA), CallRail or CallTrackingMetrics call-tracking numbers per marketing channel, and Zapier or Make automations for any platform we don't natively support.

How does TMN Creative handle service-area SEO?

Every home services site we build at TMN Creative gets full service-area schema markup, location-specific landing pages for each town or zip code you serve, Google Business Profile optimization, and local citation guidance. We also tag your service categories, service areas, and operating hours in JSON-LD so AI-assisted search systems can read your services, service areas, and proof when homeowners ask "who is the best plumber near me?" or "best HVAC company in [city]." This is the difference between showing up in legacy Google results and being easier for AI and search systems to understand.

Can a home services website capture leads after hours?

Yes, and this is one of the most underused revenue features in the trades. We build after-hours lead capture into every TMN Creative home services site: web forms that drop into your CRM, SMS-back acknowledgements, emergency CTAs that route to your on-call line, scheduling integrations that book the next available appointment slot, and Calendly or ServiceTitan booking widgets that respect your dispatch availability. Most home services businesses we work with get 30 to 50 percent of their leads outside business hours. If your current site does not capture them, your competitor's site will.

How long does it take to build a home services website?

First paid draft in 72 hours. Most home services sites are live in 7 to 10 days. Multi-trade or multi-location custom builds run 2 to 3 weeks. Trevor and Taylor Niemann work directly on every project from our studio in Fort Worth, Texas, which is why turnaround is so fast and quality is consistent across builds.

Do you support financing partners like GreenSky, Synchrony, and Service Finance?

Yes. We integrate financing CTAs and pre-qualification flows for GreenSky, Synchrony, Wells Fargo, Sunlight Financial, Service Finance Company, FTL Finance, Ygrene PACE, and other home improvement lenders. Financing pages are treated as important because financing is what closes high-ticket residential jobs (water heaters, HVAC systems, roof replacements, full landscape installs, generator installs). We design the page like a checkout flow, not a buried legal disclosure.

Do you replace Scorpion, Townsquare, BlueCorona, or Hibu sites?

Yes, frequently. Most of those platforms ship templated sites with monthly retainers, restricted code access, and conversion patterns that have not been refreshed in years. TMN Creative hand-codes every site so it is typically much leaner and faster, looks distinct (not templated), and is fully owned by you. We handle the migration, redirects, SEO continuity, and any lead-routing rewires from your old vendor. Most clients see the redirect cutover finish overnight with careful redirect and Search Console planning.

Will I own my home services website if I leave TMN Creative?

Yes. Code, copy, design files, domain, hosting access, and any third-party integrations are 100 percent yours. We hand-code every site (no proprietary CMS, no page builders, no DataPins or Scorpion lock-in) so you can take the site to any host or developer at any time. There are no retainers, no contracts, and no vendor dependencies. This is the TMN Creative standard, and it is non-negotiable on every build.
