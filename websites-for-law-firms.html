Law Firm Websites

A law firm website that earns trust before the first consultation.

Prospective clients judge your competence by your website before they ever read your case results. If your site looks like a template with stock photos of gavels and handshakes, they question whether you are the right attorney for a serious matter.

The firms that win referral business online share three things: a site that communicates seriousness and attention to detail, attorney profiles that establish real credentials, and practice area pages that rank for the searches prospective clients actually make. Most law firm websites fail on at least two of these.

Every site we build for law firms includes: individual practice area pages optimized for search, attorney profiles with credentials and bar admissions, case results and settlement highlights, client testimonials, and professional design that communicates authority without looking like every other firm in your market.

We build fast. First draft in 72 hours. Final site live within 7 days. You own everything. No retainers, no lock-in, no proprietary CMS you cannot leave.
