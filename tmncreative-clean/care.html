Common questions

A few things people ask before signing up.

How do I request an update?

Just email or text us what you need changed. We handle it and let you know when it's live. No tickets, no portals, no back and forth.

What counts as an update?

Text changes, photo swaps, adding a team member, updating hours or pricing, tweaking a headline. Anything that doesn't require building a new section or page from scratch.

What if I need more than my plan includes?

Anything beyond your monthly hours is billed at $100/hr. We will always let you know before we go over so there are no surprises.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Just let us know and we will wrap things up cleanly.

Do I have to be on a care plan?

No. You can host your site anywhere and own it outright. If you ever need something changed down the road, we are available at $100/hr.
