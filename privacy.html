Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

True Margin North, LLC ("TMN Creative," "we," "us," or "our") operates the website tmncreative.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly through our website contact form, including your name, email address, phone number (if provided), current website URL, service interest, and any additional details you share. We also collect basic analytics data through Plausible Analytics, which does not use cookies and does not collect personally identifiable information.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to respond to your inquiry, provide a site review, schedule a strategy call, deliver the services you purchase, and send follow-up communications related to your inquiry or project. We may also use your phone number to send SMS messages if you have opted in through our website form.

SMS/Text Messaging

If you provide your phone number and check the SMS consent box on our contact form, you agree to receive text messages from True Margin North, LLC related to your inquiry or service request. These messages may include appointment confirmations, follow-up communications, and customer care messages.

Message frequency varies based on your interactions. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out of text messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. For help, reply HELP or contact us at [email protected].

Your consent to receive SMS messages is not a condition of purchasing any goods or services. We will not share your phone number or SMS consent information with any third parties for marketing purposes.

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your information with service providers who assist us in operating our business (such as our CRM platform, email service, and SMS messaging provider), but only to the extent necessary to provide our services. These providers are not permitted to use your information for their own marketing purposes.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses Plausible Analytics, a privacy-focused analytics tool that does not use cookies and does not collect personally identifiable information. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking scripts.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us at [email protected]. You may also opt out of SMS messages by replying STOP to any text message.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
