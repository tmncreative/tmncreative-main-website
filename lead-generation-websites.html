What makes a website actually generate leads.

Lead generation is not a single feature — it is a system. Hero hierarchy, form friction, CTA placement, conversion tracking, retargeting, and CRM routing all have to work together.

01

Hero CTA architecture

One primary action above the fold. Clear value statement, one-line proof, primary CTA, and a fallback secondary CTA for not-yet-ready visitors. No carousel sliders. No competing options.

02

Multi-step & smart forms

Multi-step forms with progress indicators outperform long single-page forms by 30 to 60 percent. We build conditional logic, smart field defaults, and inline validation. Form abandonment tracked.

03

A/B testing & CRO

VWO, Optimizely, Convert.com, or GA4 experiments. We test hero headline, primary CTA copy, form length, and social proof first because those drive the largest lifts.

04

Lead magnets & offers

Downloadable PDFs, checklists, calculators, assessments, gated case studies. We build the magnet, the opt-in flow, the auto-delivery email, and the nurture sequence behind it.

FAQ

Common questions about lead generation websites.

Answers to what we hear from operators when they reach out about lead gen builds.

What is a lead generation website?

A lead generation website is a site engineered for one job: turn anonymous visitors into identified leads who hand over their contact information so you can sell to them. Unlike a brochure site, a lead gen site is built around conversion. Every page has a primary CTA, every form is optimized to reduce friction, every visit is tracked through GA4, Meta Pixel, and Google Ads, and every captured lead flows into a CRM and nurture sequence. TMN Creative builds lead generation websites starting at $2,250.

What conversion rate should a lead generation website achieve?

Industry benchmarks vary widely by traffic source. Cold organic traffic on a B2B service site typically converts at 1.5 to 3 percent. Warm traffic from email or referrals converts at 5 to 10 percent. Paid traffic on a dedicated landing page should hit 5 to 12 percent if the offer is strong. Home services, legal, and dental sites often see 6 to 10 percent on local searcher traffic. Our hand-coded lead gen builds typically lift conversion rates by 30 to 80 percent over a templated WordPress or Wix site because of faster load times, better hierarchy, fewer form friction points, and proper CRO.

How do you set up GA4, Meta Pixel, and Google Ads conversion tracking?

Every site we build ships with GA4, Plausible, Meta Pixel, Google Ads conversion tracking, and LinkedIn Insight Tag wired up by default. We define conversion events for every meaningful action — form submits, calendar bookings, phone clicks, lead-magnet downloads, scroll depth, and outbound clicks. Conversions fire as both client-side and server-side events where appropriate, and we set up Conversions API for Meta to recover signal lost to iOS 14.5+ tracking restrictions. You see real attribution, not the partial picture most sites give you.

Which CRM integrations do you support for lead routing?

We integrate with HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, Zoho CRM, Close, Copper, Monday CRM, ActiveCampaign, Mailchimp, ConvertKit, Brevo, Constant Contact, Beehiiv, BoomTown, Sierra Interactive, CINC, kvCORE, Follow Up Boss, and Wise Agent. Forms route to the CRM via native API or Zapier/Make where needed. We also support webhook routing for custom CRMs. Lead source, UTMs, page URL, and referrer all pass through to the CRM record so attribution stays clean.

Do you run A/B tests and split tests?

Yes. For Custom Studio Builds we set up A/B testing with Google Optimize alternatives like VWO, Optimizely, Convert.com, or simple custom split tests using GA4 experiments. We typically test the hero headline, the primary CTA copy, the form length (2-field versus 5-field), and the social proof section first because those drive the largest conversion rate lifts. We document every test and report on statistical significance before declaring a winner.

Are TMN Creative lead generation sites structured for Google AI features and ChatGPT?

Yes — AI-search foundations are built into every site by default. We mark up your services with Service schema, your business with Organization schema, and your FAQ content with FAQPage schema so AI engines can parse your pages more reliably. We also write service and offer pages in the question-answer format that ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, and Google AI Overviews prefer. AI-driven traffic converts at higher rates than typical Google traffic because users have already qualified themselves through their conversation with the AI.

Can you build retargeting and nurture sequences?

Yes. We set up Meta retargeting audiences, Google Ads remarketing lists, LinkedIn matched audiences, and TikTok custom audiences from your site traffic and form submitters. We also configure email nurture sequences in your ESP — typically a 7 to 14-day sequence for new leads with educational content, social proof, and a clear path back to a booking page. Custom Studio Builds can include behavioral triggering (visited pricing twice, downloaded lead magnet but did not book) for sharper segmentation.
